You Asked, We Answered

What makes Sunny Kindergarten special?Sunny Kindergarten provides a balanced approach between structured learning and free play, helping children grow naturally and confidently..

  • Is there a trial class available? Yes! We offer trial days so children can experience our environment before enrolling. Parents are also allowed to see around the kindergarten for the first time.

  • How do you ensure children's safety?. Safety is our top priority. Our classrooms are secure, and children are always supervised by trained staff.

  • What activities do children do daily? Our daily activities include structured learning sessions, free play, arts and crafts, outdoor exploration, storytelling, and music and movement. We also require students to have a rest periods to recharge and maintain their energy levels.

  • Do you communicate with parents regularly? Yes, we provide regular updates and reports to keep informed parents about their child's progress.
