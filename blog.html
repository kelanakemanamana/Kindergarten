A Day At Sunny Kindergarten!

Today at Sunny KindergartenToday was such a happy day at Sunny Kindergarten ! 🌈 In the morning, the children came in with big smiles. We started our day by sitting in a circle and singing our favorite songs together 🎶 Everyone clapped, laughed, and said “good morning” to their friends. After that, it was time to play outside! The children ran around, played games, and enjoyed the sunshine ☀️ Some played on the swings, some chased bubbles, and some just giggled with their friends. It was so much fun! When we went back inside, we had art time 🎨 The children used colorful paper, crayons, and stickers to make their own creations. Every picture was different and special—just like them! Later, we had snack time 🍎 The children sat together and shared stories while eating. They practiced saying “thank you” and “please,” and took turns nicely. Before going home, we had storytime 📖 Everyone sat quietly and listened to a fun story. Some children even guessed what would happen next! Today was full of smiles, laughter, and learning 💛 We can't wait for another fun day tomorrow at Sunny Kindergarten!
